How do I Earn Points ?

“Win points to get and keep your OCR Profile Card on the Home Page in the Top 10 Maniacs and to win monthly and annual prizes! Every time you create new content for the OM community (e.g., publish and/or share an article, start a discussion or post pictures or videos), you get Maniac points. The more points you gain, the more likely you are to win a prize. Prizes are awarded monthly, so get started contributing content and sharing today!

Here’s a convenient list of the activities and points you earn by contributing to the OCR community on obstaclemaniac.com.”

Profile Set Up
  • Complete Profile - 100 points (must include photo to get points)
  • Update your profile with a recent race - 10 points for each entry
  • Following an athlete - 5 points/athlete
  • Accepting a follower - 5 points/athlete
Articles
  • Authoring/Creating an article - 100 points
  • Sharing your article with Facebook - 50 points
  • Sharing your article with Twitter - 50 points
  • Commenting on an article - 5 points (max of 10 points awarded/article)
  • Sharing an article that someone else wrote on Facebook or Twitter 5 points each (max 20 points/article)
  • Getting a “Like” or a “Favorite” on an article you wrote - 5 point/like
Chat
  • Authoring at Chat - 50 points
  • Sharing your chat with Facebook - 25 points
  • Sharing your chat with Twitter - 25 points
  • Commenting on a chat - 5 points/comment (max of 15 points awarded/chat)
  • Sharing a chat on Facebook or Twitter 5 points each (max 15 points/chat)

About Us

ObstacleManiac has been created with one purpose in mind, to help obstacle racers become better athletes by learning from others that participate in the sport. Obstacle Course racing requires a unique set of skills and preparation. It requires strength, endurance, stamina, heart, mind, and spirit. Here at Obstaclemaniac one thing we’ve realized is that Obstacle course racers are also a unique group; a group that not only loves what we do but we love those that love to do it with us. We enjoy the spirit of the race, the team work, and the learnings we have to offer. It’s with this in mind that we’ve created Obstacle Maniac.

Obstacle Maniac is your home to not only learn what others do to train but gives you, the athlete an opportunity to share your own training and diet practices with others. Once you create a profile on Obstaclemaniac you have the opportunity to author your own articles, read and learn from other Obstaclemaniacs, or simply chat about obstacle course racing using our “Discussion” feature. Whether you want to share your own race experiences, write a product review, or discuss training programs, it’s all here on Obstaclemaniac.

We want Obstaclemaniac to be your home for Obstacle course racing information. So please send us feedback on what we can do better. As a new company it’s our goal to bring value to you and to connect you with the information that helps you become a better athlete, a stronger warrior, and an unbeatable Spartan.

Good racing to all our Maniacs!
