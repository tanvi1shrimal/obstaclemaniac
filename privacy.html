How do I Earn Points ?

“Win points to get and keep your OCR Profile Card on the Home Page in the Top 10 Maniacs and to win monthly and annual prizes! Every time you create new content for the OM community (e.g., publish and/or share an article, start a discussion or post pictures or videos), you get Maniac points. The more points you gain, the more likely you are to win a prize. Prizes are awarded monthly, so get started contributing content and sharing today!

Here’s a convenient list of the activities and points you earn by contributing to the OCR community on obstaclemaniac.com.”

Profile Set Up
  • Complete Profile - 100 points (must include photo to get points)
  • Update your profile with a recent race - 10 points for each entry
  • Following an athlete - 5 points/athlete
  • Accepting a follower - 5 points/athlete
Articles
  • Authoring/Creating an article - 100 points
  • Sharing your article with Facebook - 50 points
  • Sharing your article with Twitter - 50 points
  • Commenting on an article - 5 points (max of 10 points awarded/article)
  • Sharing an article that someone else wrote on Facebook or Twitter 5 points each (max 20 points/article)
  • Getting a “Like” or a “Favorite” on an article you wrote - 5 point/like
Chat
  • Authoring at Chat - 50 points
  • Sharing your chat with Facebook - 25 points
  • Sharing your chat with Twitter - 25 points
  • Commenting on a chat - 5 points/comment (max of 15 points awarded/chat)
  • Sharing a chat on Facebook or Twitter 5 points each (max 15 points/chat)

Privacy

As users of social media those of us at Obstaclemaniac are dedicated to maintaining the security of our user information. As such we don’t share your personal information with any outside agency, company, sponsor, or advertiser. Our goal is to provide you with a safe, yet compelling experience that allows like minded obstaclecourse racers to share and benefit from the success, and yes, failures, of other racers.

We do collect Content: We collect the content and other information your provide when you use our services, including when you sign up for an account, create, or share, and message or communicate with others. This includes the content, photo’s, video’s, or other content you might post. All articles and associated content remains under the ownership of the user however in posting content the user provides Obstaclemanic with an binding and exclusive, perpetual, royalty free, right to use, re-use, and/or resell the content.

Information about Payments; If you use our services for purchases or financial transactions (like when you buy something on Obstaclemaniac or purchase a sponsored article), we collect information about the purchase transaction. This includes your payment information, such as your credit or debt card number and other card information, and other account and authentication information, as well as billing, shipping, and contact details.

The Data We Collect We Use to Improve our service and drive sponsorship: As a tool in aiding our sponsors we do collect general data on the users of Obstaclemaniac. We don’t share any user names or individual specific data. We share general data about the obstaclemaniac community which is intended to provide our sponsors with a broad understanding of our users such as location, age, gender, experience, and income ranges.

We Do Use Your Data To Communicate With You: We will on occasion distribute newsletters or emails and we will use data you provided when setting up your profile to communicate directly with you regarding Obstaclemaniac, changes in our service, or new offers. We may also use your personal information to communicate with you regarding use of Obstaclemaniac if there are any Terms Of Use Violations.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy: We will notify all users before we make any changes to our Privacy Policy and give all users an opportunity to review and comment before enacting any planned change.
