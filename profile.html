BASIC INFO

I have been hooked on OCR since my first Spartan Race (Brisbane, June 2013). I was carrying an injury coming in to the event so I put my hand up to be an obstacle volunteer, so I could at least be a part of the day that I had been training for. Plus the free race for volunteering was a nice bonus. I had more fun than I’d had in years, both while volunteering and out on the course for my own run.

Since that day I have volunteered and run in an obstacle race about every 4 weeks. I love getting “behind the scenes”. Volunteering has connected me to the wonderful OCR community.

OCR has changed me from an unhealthy out of shape couch potato to the point where I no longer cringe if I see a photo of myself with my shirt off. I want to make sure that these events stick around so they can be an incentive for other people, as they were for me. Pay it forward.
