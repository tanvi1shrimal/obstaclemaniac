How do I Earn Points ?

“Win points to get and keep your OCR Profile Card on the Home Page in the Top 10 Maniacs and to win monthly and annual prizes! Every time you create new content for the OM community (e.g., publish and/or share an article, start a discussion or post pictures or videos), you get Maniac points. The more points you gain, the more likely you are to win a prize. Prizes are awarded monthly, so get started contributing content and sharing today!

Here’s a convenient list of the activities and points you earn by contributing to the OCR community on obstaclemaniac.com.”

Profile Set Up
  • Complete Profile - 100 points (must include photo to get points)
  • Update your profile with a recent race - 10 points for each entry
  • Following an athlete - 5 points/athlete
  • Accepting a follower - 5 points/athlete
Articles
  • Authoring/Creating an article - 100 points
  • Sharing your article with Facebook - 50 points
  • Sharing your article with Twitter - 50 points
  • Commenting on an article - 5 points (max of 10 points awarded/article)
  • Sharing an article that someone else wrote on Facebook or Twitter 5 points each (max 20 points/article)
  • Getting a “Like” or a “Favorite” on an article you wrote - 5 point/like
Chat
  • Authoring at Chat - 50 points
  • Sharing your chat with Facebook - 25 points
  • Sharing your chat with Twitter - 25 points
  • Commenting on a chat - 5 points/comment (max of 15 points awarded/chat)
  • Sharing a chat on Facebook or Twitter 5 points each (max 15 points/chat)

OM Point Series - Beta

At Obstaclemaniac we're always about the community - a community that spans more than just the elite athletes. So, when it comes to our points series we track elite athletes and leaders by age group. Our point series begins on January 1st of every year. The current point totals reflect a start date of January 1st, 2015.

Races We Currently Track: We track races that report statistics by age group. This will expand as we improve our tools.

- Spartan Race
- BattleFrog
- Atlas Race

How We Award Points: ObstacleManiac "normalizes" points across elite and open races. If you run elite, but don't place in the top 5, your time rolls over to the age-group point series. If however you run elite and place in the top 5, your points are awarded for elite only. Racers can only be awarded points for elite or open - not both in any single race. For age groups, we calculate points based on your name and age when you sign up for races. We realize that there are cases where an individual's birthday falls into two age groups during a year (a 24 year old on Jan 1, 2015 turning 25 in August). Points will be awarded in a single age group determined by your age on Jan 1st of each year.

Elite Points:

1st - 5 points; 2nd - 4 points; 3rd - 3 points; 4th - 2 points; 5th - 1 point

Age Group Points (Under 20, 20-24, 25-29; 30-34; 35-39; 40-44; 45-49; 50-54; 55-59; 60 and up)

1st - 5 points; 2nd - 4 points; 3rd - 3 points; 4th - 2 points; 5th - 1 point

Each year represents a new start and there are no point carryovers. This gives everyone a fresh opportunity to be on top of their age groups. The more races you run, the more points you earn. So get your OCR on!

We're always looking for feedback on our model so don't hold back. Tell us what you think!?
